Your Privacy Rights

Under the CCPA, you have the right to:

  • Know what categories of personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell
  • Request access to the specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of your personal information

Submit Your Request

Please email us at info@vrume.com with the subject line: "CCPA Request" and include the following:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address (and/or other identifying information)
  • The type of request (Access / Deletion / Opt-Out)

We will verify your identity and respond within 45 days as required by law.
