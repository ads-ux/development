Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 28, 2025

At Vrume.com, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Name and email address
  • Company name and job title
  • IP address and browser/device information
  • Usage data (how you interact with our website)
  • Cookies and tracking technologies (for analytics, advertising, and personalization)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process your requests and respond to inquiries
  • Send marketing and promotional communications
  • Analyze traffic and user behavior
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

Sharing of Information

We may share your information with:

  • Trusted service providers assisting in operations (e.g., email platforms, analytics providers)
  • Business partners and affiliates (only where permitted by law)
  • Government authorities when legally required

We do not routinely sell your personal information to third parties. However, in certain circumstances — such as business partnerships, lead generation programs, or marketing collaborations — we may share or sell limited personal information where permitted by law and with appropriate user consent.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Understand site usage
  • Personalize your experience
  • Provide targeted advertising

You can control cookie settings through your browser at any time.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR, CCPA, or other privacy laws, including:

  • Requesting access to your personal data
  • Requesting correction or deletion of your data
  • Objecting to certain uses of your data
  • Managing your communication preferences

To submit a request, please visit:

  • GDPR Data Request
  • CCPA Privacy Request

Or email us at info@vrume.com.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

International Users

If you access our services from outside Canada or the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in these countries.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective Date."

Data Sharing and Selling

At Vrume, we value transparency regarding how we handle your personal information.

We do not routinely sell your personal information to third parties. However, we may share or sell limited categories of personal information where permitted by law, including:

  • Business partnerships and marketing collaborations
  • Lead generation programs
  • Affiliate and advertising partnerships

Any such sharing or selling is conducted:

  • With appropriate user consent where required by law

If you wish to opt out of the sale of your personal information, you may do so at any time by visiting: Manage My Privacy Choices

You can also submit a request by emailing us at info@vrume.com.
