AdTech Entity Definitions

Member Area Traffic: High-intent ad inventory served to users who are actively logged into dating platforms — browsing profiles and engaging with content.

CPC (Cost Per Click): Pricing model where the advertiser pays a fixed amount per click received.

CPA (Cost Per Acquisition): The total cost divided by the number of conversions, indicating the efficiency of the campaign.

Conversion Rate (CR): The percentage of clicks that result in a desired action (registration, sign-up, etc.).

EPC (Earnings Per Click): Total revenue divided by total clicks — a key metric for evaluating traffic profitability.

Secondary Traffic Lanes: High-intent inventory sources including members-area tabs and direct link placements inside active dating platforms.
