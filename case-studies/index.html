AdTech Entity Definitions

CPL (Cost Per Lead): A performance-based advertising model where advertisers pay for every lead generated.

ROI (Return on Investment): A metric used to evaluate the efficiency of an investment, calculated as (Revenue - Cost) / Cost.

Secondary Traffic Lanes: Proprietary, non-social inventory sources including members-area tabs, email-passing clicks, and direct-intent redirects.

GEO (Geographic Targeting): The process of identifying and targeting potential customers based on their physical location.
