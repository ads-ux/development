🧠 Norway Dating Case Study: Winning Segments

  • Top Sub-IDs: 9424 (+58% ROI, 24.75% CVR), 16810 (+93% ROI, 33.33% CVR), 48099 (+300% ROI, 50% CVR).
  • Devices: Android and iOS both proved profitable – iOS slightly ahead on EPC, Android stronger on volume.

AdTech Entity Definitions

ROI (Return on Investment): Efficiency metric for advertising spend, (Revenue - Cost) / Cost.
