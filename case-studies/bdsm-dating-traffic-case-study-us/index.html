AdTech Entity Definitions

CPL (Cost Per Lead): Performance advertising model where payment is triggered by a lead action.

Secondary Traffic Lanes: High-intent inventory sources including members-area tabs and email-passing redirects.

ROI (Return on Investment): Efficiency metric for advertising spend, (Revenue - Cost) / Cost.
