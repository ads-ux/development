🧠 Winning Segments Inside the Nordics Campaign

  • GEO: Denmark was the star performer with a $6.45 eCPA and 7.76% CR, making it the first GEO to scale. Sweden and Finland added solid incremental volume at slightly higher eCPAs.
  • Device: Desktop traffic delivered a $5.89 eCPA and 8.49% CR, outperforming mobile on efficiency. Mobile kept the funnel fed with extra clicks and conversions.
  • Browsers: Chrome carried the campaign with 97 conversions and a $6.25 eCPA, while Safari Mobile and the Facebook in-app browser added profitable pockets of volume.
  • Operating Systems: Older Android versions, iOS 18, Windows 10, and MacOS 15 Sequoia all contributed strong results — exactly what you’d expect from a 40+ audience browsing from home devices.
  • Languages: Danish, Swedish, and Finnish users showed clear local intent, confirming that Vrume’s Nordic email lists are built from real regional traffic, not mixed or spoofed GEOs.
