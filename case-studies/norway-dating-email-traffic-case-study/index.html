AdTech Entity Definitions

DOI (Double Opt-In): A conversion model requiring the user to confirm registration twice — email click + email confirmation — ensuring high-quality, validated leads.

Email Passing Traffic: High-intent clicks from logged-in dating platform members delivered via email CTA — pre-warmed, intent-driven, and structurally superior to display traffic.

eCPA (Effective Cost Per Acquisition): Actual cost per conversion recorded during the campaign, calculated as total spend divided by total conversions.

ROI (Return on Investment): Advertising efficiency metric: (Revenue - Cost) / Cost × 100.

CPL (Cost Per Lead): Performance advertising model where payment is triggered by a lead submission action.

Secondary Traffic Lanes: High-intent inventory sources including members-area tabs and email-passing redirects from active dating site users.
