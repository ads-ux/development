🧠 Winning Segments Inside the Nordics BBW Campaign

  • GEO: Denmark delivered 119 conversions with a $3.26 eCPA and 15.32% CR. Sweden and Finland provided additional profitable volume.
  • Device: Desktop traffic led performance with a $3.62 eCPA and 13.82% CR. Mobile contributed 1,037 clicks at a $6.73 eCPA.
  • Device Manufacturers: Samsung delivered standout results with a $2.73 eCPA and 18.31% CR. Motorola produced a remarkable $1.30 eCPA and 38.46% CR.
  • Carriers: WiFi users generated the bulk of conversions (139 conversions) at a $5.03 eCPA. Denmark’s Telenor carrier performed strongly with a $4.25 eCPA and 11.76% CR, while Finnish carriers Elisa, DNA, and Telia all contributed steady performance.
  • Ad Zones: One premium email zone generated 86 conversions at just $1.57 eCPA. Multiple premium zones produced CRs over 20%, making them ideal for BBW dating scale.
